# Velocita-Spec-Track-Models
This is a repository of a selection of Images from every track model that I have worked on for Velocita Spec.
Velocita Spec is a racing management game that I spearheaded and develop with a team of about 10 people.
My role is General Oversight and Lead Modeler, where I modeled most of the tracks in the game.

Some tracks are simply refurbished versions of basic models made by other people, with extra trackside objects and some minor layout adjustments. 
These will be marked in their respective folders with a [+].
